About Me

I am an Assistant Professor of Statistics at the University of Rome "Tor Vergata". I hold an MSc in Econometrics and a PhD in Statistics. My research focuses on developing new statistical tools, especially from a Bayesian perspective, with applications in economics, finance, social sciences, and medicine. I have a strong passion for both statistics and football.

Research Interests

  • Mixture Models
  • Latent Markov Models
  • Inference for Stochastic Processes
  • Copula Models
  • Applied Bayesian Modelling
